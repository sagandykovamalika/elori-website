1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Contact us for support or with questions.
  • Provide feedback or review our App.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including the hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Diagnostics and Crash Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics, a Google service, to help us understand app crashes, performance issues, and stability problems. Crash reports may include device and operating system information, app version, timestamps, crash traces, diagnostic identifiers, and related technical data needed to diagnose the issue.
  • Usage and App State Data: We may collect limited information about app activity and app state needed to provide, maintain, secure, and improve the Service.

C. Content, Templates, and Firebase Services

Elori uses Firebase and Google Cloud services to deliver and operate parts of the App. This includes Firestore-backed configuration or content records and Firebase Storage or Google Cloud Storage-backed assets such as templates, examples, images, or other app content made available inside Elori. These services may process technical request information, device or app identifiers, timestamps, IP address, and content-delivery metadata as needed to provide the requested content and keep the App working reliably.

D. Local Caching and UserDefaults

Elori stores some information locally on your device using app storage, caches, and Apple UserDefaults. This may include preferences, onboarding state, entitlement or purchase state, recently loaded content metadata, cached assets, and settings that help the App remember your choices and work faster. Local data remains on your device unless removed by you, cleared by iOS, or deleted when you uninstall the App.

E. Photo Library Access and Export

To provide the core functionality of the App (editing photos and creating carousels), Elori may request access to selected photos and permission to save or export finished images to your device's photo library.

  • Local Processing: We prioritize your privacy. All photo processing and editing are designed to be performed locally on your device. We do not use your personal photos to train models, and we do not view your photo library.
  • Export Permission: If you choose to save or export a creation, iOS may ask for permission to add the exported image to your photo library. You can revoke photo permissions at any time in your device settings, though this may limit importing or exporting functionality.
